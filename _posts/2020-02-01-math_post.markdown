The average of an array, $$ x $$, is written as $$ \bar{x} $$ and it defined as:

$$ \bar{x} = \frac{1}{n} \left( \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i \right) $$ 

It can be written more explicitly as:

$$ \bar{x} = \frac{x_1+x_2+....+x_n}{x} $$
